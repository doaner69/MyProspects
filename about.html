MyProspects is built on Local Sphere — a proprietary identity and data infrastructure connecting the digital and physical worlds at a scale no standalone platform can replicate.

The problem we set out to solve.

Every company's website tells the same story: thousands of visitors, almost none identified. Marketing teams invest millions to drive traffic, then watch 95%+ of it disappear into the dark funnel with no actionable signal.

The existing solutions were built for enterprise B2B accounts with seven-figure budgets, multi-year contracts, and months-long implementations. They showed you company-level data when you needed person-level data. They served one vertical when your business crossed four.

We built MyProspects to be different: person-level resolution at SMB-accessible pricing, on month-to-month contracts, live within 48 hours, serving B2B, B2C, Recruitment, and Nonprofit under one roof — a combination rarely found in a single platform.

Powered By

Local Sphere

MyProspects is a product of Local Sphere — a company built around the belief that identity resolution should connect the digital and physical worlds, not just match cookies.

Local Sphere's infrastructure powers the offline intelligence layer that gives MyProspects its deepest competitive differentiation: the ability to layer household context onto digital identity and activate across every channel, including direct mail — a capability not available in digital-only identity platforms.

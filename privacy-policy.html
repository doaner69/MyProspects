Effective Date: April 9, 2026  ·  Last Updated: April 9, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Local Sphere, LLC ("Local Sphere," "MyProspects," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, shares, and protects information in connection with the MyProspects identity resolution and buyer intent platform, including our website at myprospects.io (the "Site") and related services (collectively, the "Services").

By using the Services, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use our Services.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

We collect information you provide directly when you:

  • Complete a demo request or contact form (name, email, company, job title, phone)
  • Subscribe to communications or newsletters
  • Enter into a subscription or service agreement
  • Communicate with our team by email or other means

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our Site, we and our service providers may automatically collect:

  • IP address, browser type, device type, and operating system
  • Pages visited, time spent, and navigation paths
  • Referring URLs and search terms
  • General geographic location derived from IP address

1.3 Identity Resolution Data (Platform Customers)

Our core service — visitor identity resolution — processes data on behalf of our business customers ("Customers"). When deployed on a Customer's website via our JavaScript pixel, our platform may identify individuals visiting that site using probabilistic and deterministic matching techniques against our identity graph. This includes:

  • Hashed email addresses and identifiers (we use SHA-256 hashing — no plaintext PII is transmitted)
  • Behavioral signals observed across the open web (intent data)
  • Household-level demographic context sourced from our Local Sphere data infrastructure
  • On-site behavior including pages viewed, session duration, and interaction patterns

In these contexts, MyProspects acts as a data processor on behalf of our Customers, who are the data controllers responsible for their own end-user privacy disclosures. We encourage all Customers to maintain a transparent privacy policy on their own sites.

We use the information we collect to:

  • Detect, prevent, and address fraud or security incidents
  • Comply with legal obligations

5. Data Security

We implement administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your information, including:

  • AES-256 encryption at rest
  • TLS 1.3 encryption in transit

  • SOC 2 Type II certified security controls (availability, confidentiality, processing integrity)

No method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.
